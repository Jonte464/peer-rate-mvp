En snabbfil för människor online.

PeerRate standardiserar betyg och kopplar dem till riktiga affärer — så att förtroende blir mätbart, portabelt och rättvist.

Verified

PeerRate reviews are tied to real work relationships, not anonymous opinions. Every review includes who the reviewer is, how they worked with you, and when. Once submitted, feedback is locked and can’t be edited — ensuring credibility, fairness, and resistance to fake or inflated reviews.

Portable

Your PeerRate profile isn’t tied to a single employer or platform. It moves with you across roles, companies, and career stages. You can share it wherever professional trust matters — with hiring managers, clients, or collaborators — without asking for new references each time.

Explainable

PeerRate shows why you’re trusted, not just that you are. Every score is backed by clear context, structured criteria, and optional written feedback. This makes your reputation understandable, defensible, and useful — not a black-box rating.
